Zedbazi




Zedbazi is an Iranian hip hop band, formed by longtime friends Saman Wilson, Mehrad Hidden, Sohrab Mj from Tehran, Later joined by Alireza Jj, Sijal & Nassim. They are known as the pioneer of gangsta rap in Iran. They also credited with marking the beginning of a new movement in Iranian music.
